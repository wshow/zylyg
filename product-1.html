Type II Urea Blast Media


Plastic abrasive beads are commonly used for paint stripping, coatings removal, pressure blasting, powder coating, deflashing, deburring, surface preparation, and mold cleaning. Plastic abrasive media is used for paint removal and refurbishing steel, aluminum, rubber, composites, cars, trucks, airplanes, helicopters, boats, and motorcycles. Plastic Abrasive is available in all standard sizes for deflashing & deburring applications: Cryogenic Deflashing Media, Polycarbonate, Nylon Polyamide, and Polystyrene Beads. Blasting with Plastic Media avoids damage to delicate substrates thus a superb alternative to hard abrasive materials also as chemical stripping. Plastic abrasive is often reclaimed repeatedly, offering excellent cost benefits.
